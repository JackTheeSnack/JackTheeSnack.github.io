What is Internet Culture?

When the internet began to take off in the world, a culture began to grow. Communities formed and it continued to expand. This culture centers around communication, entertainment, and business, which correlates with the original intention of the internet.


How Has It Affected People?

The use of technology has allowed for the improvement in the world as a whole. The key aspects of the culture have only gotten easier with the rise of technology. Communication has become effortless with people only having to press a button, allowing for everyonoe to be able to speak without having to go through all the troulbe. Entertainment is more accessible and don't even have to watch it on their TV since it is already somewhere on the internet. Business has also been affected since the information that is now accrssible is incredibly useful for said business.

Examples of The Culture

Memes

One of the more comedic aspects of the internet, memes have grown across all culture simply meant to induce a laugh to the viewer.

Social Media

Social media is one example of the improvement of communication throughout the last decades. Before we had letters, now we have texting and social media, which provides an easier way to communicate with others. This boost in communication allows for so much information to be shared to people no matter the distance between them.
